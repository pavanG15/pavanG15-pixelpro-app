Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PixelPro handles your data. PixelPro is built with a privacy-first approach and does not collect, store, or upload any personal information.

1. No Image Uploading

PixelPro processes all images locally on your device. No photos are uploaded to any server. Your files remain 100% private.

2. No Data Collection

PixelPro does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Device information
  • Location
  • Images or files
  • Usage analytics

3. Optional Ads

If ads are enabled in the app, third-party ad providers (like Google AdSense / AdMob) may show ads based on their own policies. PixelPro does not track or share any data.

4. Cookies

PixelPro does not use cookies. If you open external links (e.g., FAQ, support), those websites may use cookies.

5. External Links

Some pages may contain external links. PixelPro is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

6. Children's Privacy

PixelPro does not collect any data from children or adults. The app is safe for all age groups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when required. Updates will be shown on this same page.
